About

Hi, I'm Corey. I'm a graphic designer focused on athletics. I graduated from the University of Maine in 2024 with a major in Marketing and a minor in Graphic Design, and I've been doing it ever since. I'm currently with Dartmouth College Athletics. My work covers posters, social media graphics, motion graphics, and photography, which you can see on my portfolio. Take a look at my experience below.
